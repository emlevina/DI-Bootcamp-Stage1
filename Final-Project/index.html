Biography

Elizaveta was born on June 1, 1998 in Yekaterinburg. In 2005, Liza entered the first grade at the MAOU secondary school No. 32 with in-depth study of individual subjects (music, fine arts, literature, Moscow Art College), where she studied for 9 years, receiving general and musical education in the piano class; in addition, she attended a ballet club. As a child, Lisa wrote poetry and did not think about her own musical creativity.

In 2014, Elizaveta entered the tenth grade already at the SSCC of UrFU. After graduating from eleven grades, in 2016, Lisa entered the specialty "Production" at VGIK for correspondence courses. The reason why Lisa chose VGIK was her love for classical cinema. The choice of the specialty "Production" was based on the fact that at this faculty one can also acquire knowledge in the field of economics and law, and Elizaveta preferred the correspondence form of study, because she would like to have a more or less free schedule to work.
